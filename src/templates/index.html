    Doncic is positioned well to win rookie of the year this season, if history is any indication.

    Of the last ten players to win rookie of the year, seven finished first in either or both of our traditional or advanced ranking system, and all finished in the top five in both. Only Derrick Rose (2008-09), Andrew Wiggins(2014-15) and Malcom Brogdon (2016-17) didn’t top their rookie classes in either of the two rankings.

    Doncic currently leads in the traditional statical rankings and is second in the advanced rankings. And while rating in the top five against peers in our metric historically doesn’t guarantee taking home the rookie of the year award, it does put Doncic prominently in the conversation.
